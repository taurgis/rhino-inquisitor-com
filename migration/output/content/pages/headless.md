If your organization is looking for a way to improve its online presence and connect with more customers, Headless may be a good fit. Headless can help you create a more modern and responsive website and enhance customer engagement and conversions.

Headless commerce can provide a better user experience. In a traditional eCommerce setup, the front and back end are often tightly coupled, making it difficult to change the front end without affecting the back end.

However, with a headless commerce setup, the front end can be developed independently of the back end, allowing for a more customized and personalized user experience.
